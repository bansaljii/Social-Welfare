About Us

In Delhi, before the year 1959, welfare services for the neglected and weaker section of the society were not operating in an organized and coordinated manner. There was an urgent need to make the welfare services comprehensive and diverse in nature. Keeping in view this fact, in the year 1959, the Directorate of Social Welfare was established in Delhi.
